glyph lookup with NO OpenType shaping — Devanagari conjuncts and matras
render DECOMPOSED (each codepoint's nominal glyph in sequence: e.g.
`क्षत्रिय` appears as its constituent codepoints side by side), not as
shaped ligatures. These goldens capture that decomposed-but-real
rendering; the requirement satisfied is "no tofu", not typographic
shaping. Devanagari tofu: gone. CJK tofu: gone (all 9 fixture codepoints
covered by the committed subset). Font provenance + sha256:
`.shux/fixtures/fonts/README.md` and `evidence-manifest.json`
(`fixture_fonts`).

A focused QA re-pass must inspect the re-minted `contact-sheet.png` and
the full-resolution goldens before this record flips back to APPROVED.

## Cross-arch posture

Per the P0 spike decision (§17: SHARED goldens, empirically proven by
`make test-vt-corpus` running cross-arch against macOS-arm64-minted
goldens), these were minted on macOS-arm64 (see `evidence-manifest.json` →
`platform`) and are the SHARED (not per-arch) baselines. The fixture fonts
are byte-pinned in the repo, so the cross-arch determinism argument is
unchanged. If a future toolchain bump breaks cross-arch identity,
re-baselining is a LENS-TEST-CHANGE event.
